About

Prometheus was conceived out of an analysis of the fractional reserve banking system. The fractional reserve banking system as a system has almost perfect accounting mechanisms but is completely flawed in its centralized issuance. This redistributes exponential debt to all its participants. Furthermore its lack of real world commodities as backing make it impractical for measuring the value of goods. Its value is only "hype". Nations have been scammed into virtually global participation in a scheme to reposess the value and power of their populace through inflation.

Humanity exists in a post Bitcoin world. Currency can be created and issued by anyone who can conceive of a token system. Therefore participation in unfair systems is only currently being supported by ignorance of alternatives, ease of use, widespread adoption, and technical barriers. Bitcoin and similar technology contain technical hurdles that make it hard to participate in and unscalable for all commercial purposes.

Prometheus aims at bridging the gap of the aforementioned barriers to use with concepts drawn from social networking. Time is the unit by which all things are measured against either implicitly or explicitly making it the perfect commodity for measuring goods and valuing currency. A central bank issues notes of currency as a promise of the bank to tender its value to the holder of the note. In very much the same way, Sparks, the currency of Prometheus, is a promise of a member to provide value relative to time to the holder of the sparks. Sparks represent -1 second of a member's time to be shared with other member. Commitments of time to other people is an interaction humans make with ease every day for both commercial and interpersonal interactions. Now how humanity spends its time and what it spends it on is the only currency it will need.

As a social network Prometheus will provide the ability for members to connect and interact on a commercial level and broker transactions. By using smart contracts as a mean of solidifying transactions members can deterministically decide how their money moves at their discretion. Members will buy, sell, trade, and fund products in any way they can perceive and represent in code. Tools like Craigslist, Ebay, Kickstarter, and Patreon currently exhibit similar functionality but Prometheus' functionality is determined by its user base making its potential completely open.

Prometheus is developed in completely open source to ensure its accountability and to be resilient to change and the commercial needs of its members.

What is Prometheus?

Prometheus is a social network for commerce. Payment can only be made in the most valuable commodity in existence, time. Prometheus is a platform for a new type of economy, a volunteerist economy. This economy is backed by the promise to provide value from its members in the form of goods and services.

Time is the most valuable thing you own

There are 86,500 seconds in a day.How will you spend them.

Time as currency

There are 86,500 (sparks) in a day.How will you spend money. We have taken the concepts laid out by time banking a step further and create a decentralized monetary system that uses time to control the money supply. Employing the same communal spirit in a way that transcends local limitations and can be used globally. As long as you have time to give you have money!
